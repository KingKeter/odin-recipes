Chili Recipe

Description

This chili recipe is a hearty and flavorful dish that's perfect for warming up on a cold day. Made with ground beef, beans, tomatoes, and a blend of spices, it's a classic comfort food that everyone will love. Whether you're serving it for a weeknight dinner or a weekend gathering, this chili is sure to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Pinto Beans
  • Kidney Beans
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Green Chiles
  • Chili Powder
  • Cumin

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  2. In a large slow cooker, add all the ingredients. Add water to reach your desired consistency.
  3. OPTIONAL. Add your favorite toppings, such as shredded cheese, sour cream, or chopped green onions.
