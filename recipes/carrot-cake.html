Carrot Cake Recipe

Description

This carrot cake is a deliciously moist and flavorful dessert that's perfect for any occasion. Made with grated carrots, warm spices, and a rich cream cheese frosting, it's a classic treat that never goes out of style.

Ingredients

  • Raw Carrots
  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Ground Ginger
  • Ground Nutmeg
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Pineapple
  • Coconut Flakes
  • Pecans
  • Cream Cheese Frosting

Instructions

  1. Grate the carrots. Peel the carrots, then grate them until you have 3 1/2 cups Cover and refrigerate.
  2. Make the carrot puree. Chop the remaining carrot and steam them for 10-15 minutes. Transfer the steamed carrots to a food processor and process until smooth.
  3. Prep. Preheat oven to 350°F, line the bottoms of three 8-inch cake pans with parchment paper circles, and grease the sides.
  4. Combine the dry ingredients. Whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and spices.
  5. Cream. Beat together the butter, oil, sugars, and vanilla extract until light in color and fluffy.
  6. Add the eggs. Mix in the eggs one at a time followed by the egg white.
  7. Put it all together. Mix half of the dry ingredients into the batter followed by the carrot puree. Mix in the remaining dry ingredients.
  8. Add the mix-ins. Stir in the grated carrots, pineapple, coconut, pecans, and/or raisins.
  9. Bake. Divide the batter between the cake pans and bake for 29-32 minutes.
  10. Cool. Allow the cakes to cool in their pans for about 10 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  11. Assemble. Cut the domes from the tops of the cakes and place the first cake on a serving plate. Spread 1 cup of frosting over the top and then add the second cake layer and spread another cup of frosting over the top. Stack the final cake layer on top.
  12. Frost. Frost the outside of the cake.
