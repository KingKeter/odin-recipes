Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

A classic grilled cheese sandwich is a simple yet satisfying meal that's perfect for any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • Grated mozzarella cheese
  • Grated cheddar cheese
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Top one piece of bread with the grated cheeses.
  3. Place the other piece of bread on top, buttered side down.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and place the sandwich in the pan.
  5. Cook until golden brown on both sides, about 3-5 minutes per side.
